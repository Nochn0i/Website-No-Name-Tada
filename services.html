    • What I offer.

    Considered, black-and-white documentary work — made in the field, printed by hand, and built to outlast a feed.

    Services

    Every project starts the same way: with time spent looking before a single frame is made. Below are the four ways I currently work with clients, from single-day editorial assignments to long-form documentary portraiture. If your project doesn't fit neatly into one of these, get in touch — most of the best work has started as an exception.

    01

    Documentary Portraiture

    An unhurried sitting — in your space, in available light — built around gesture and stillness rather than direction. The aim is a portrait that reads as evidence of a person, not a performance of one.

    • Half-day session, on location
    • Contact sheet delivered within one week
    • 10 fully retouched selects, silver-gelatin toned

    02

    Editorial & Press Assignments

    Fast-turnaround coverage for publications and organizations who need images that hold their own next to strong writing — reportage-led, low intervention, delivered on deadline.

    • Single or multi-day assignments
    • Same-day preview selects
    • Licensed for print and digital editorial use

    03

    Fine-Art Prints & Exhibitions

    Archival pigment prints from the studio's ongoing bodies of work, produced in limited runs. Available framed or unframed, and by commission for exhibition and private collection.

    • Editions of 15, hand-numbered and signed
    • Archival cotton rag, 300gsm
    • Custom sizing on request

    04

    Workshops & Mentorship

    Small-group field workshops and one-to-one portfolio mentorship for photographers developing a long-term documentary practice — focused on editing, sequencing, and finding a sustained subject.

    • Groups capped at six participants
    • One field day, one review day
    • Ongoing mentorship available by arrangement

    Rates on request.

    Every project is scoped individually — day rate, usage, and location all shift the number. Get in touch with a brief description of what you're working on and I'll reply within two business days.
